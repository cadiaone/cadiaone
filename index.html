How Cadia works

Three steps to never losing track of the people who matter.

1

Tap record, just talk

After any conversation, hit the Record button and speak naturally. Tell Cadia what you discussed, what to follow up on, when you last connected.

2

AI builds your Action Plan

Cadia extracts people, saves memories, classifies Loops as one-time or recurring, and shows you an editable summary before anything is saved.

3

Pulse surfaces what's next

Your home feed keeps every open Loop in view, sorted by urgency. Stay proactive without the mental overhead of remembering it all yourself.
